    Reference: Skills

    Necromancer: Basic

    Wraith Mastery

    • Requirements: None

    Increases wraith normal attack damage, and the chance of them inflicting paralysis with their normal attacks. As a reminder, wraith attacks deal a base of 100% damage and have a base infliction chance of 1%.

    The damage and base infliction chance values shown below are the values after the bonus is applied.

    Level12345678910
    Damage110%115%120%125%145%152%159%166%173%200%
    Base Chance1%1.2%1.4%1.6%2.6%2.85%3.1%3.35%3.6%5%

    Summon Wraith

    • Requirements: None
    • Body parts used: Head

    Consumes 15% of the user's current HP to create a wraith with increased maximum HP. Can be used in the Labyrinth.

    Has no speed modifier at all levels.

    Level12345678910
    TP Cost5812
    Max HP Bonus5%8%11%14%39%43%47%51%55%80%

    Life Exchange

    • Requirements: Summon Wraith level 2
    • Body parts used: Head

    Destroys one wraith. Restores all party members' HP.

    Has an 80% speed modifier at all levels.

    Level12345678910
    TP Cost121824
    Healing Power130%135%140%1445%190%195%200%205%210%270%

    Wraith Cry

    • Requirements: None
    • Body parts used: Head

    Destroys one wraith. Reduces one enemy's defense for a set amount of turns.

    Has no speed modifier at all levels.

    Level12345678910
    TP Cost61015
    Defense Reduction14%17%20%23%26%29%32%35%
    Duration456

    Soul Candy

    • Requirements: None
    • Body parts used: Head

    Destroys one wraith. Increases all party members' elemental defense for a set amount of turns.

    Has no speed modifier at all levels.

    Level12345678910
    TP Cost61015
    Defense Increase20%22%24%26%28%30%32%34%
    Duration456

    Fierce Shield

    • Requirements: Wraith Cry level 2, Soul Candy level 2
    • Body parts used: Head

    Targets one wraith. The selected wraith will take all damage to the party until the end of the turn. The wraith under the effect of Fierce Shield has a modifier applied to all damage it takes.

    Has standard priority.

    Level12345678910
    TP Cost358
    Damage Multiplier1.5x1.45x1.4x1.35x1x0.97x0.94x0.91x0.88x0.75x

    Gravekeeping

    • Requirements: None

    If the user does not take any damage for one turn, they have a chance to summon a wraith at the end of the turn.

    Level12345678910
    Chance10%11%12%13%17%18%19%20%21%25%

    Reincarnation

    • Requirements: Gravekeeping level 3

    When a wraith is destroyed by a Necromancer skill in-battle, the user has a chance to summon another wraith.

    Level12345678910
    Chance15%16%17%18%23%24%25%26%27%33%

    Poison Bomb

    • Requirements: None
    • Body parts used: Head

    Destroys one wraith. Attempts to inflict poison on all enemies.

    Has no speed modifier at all levels.

    Flame Bomb

    • Requirements: None
    • Body parts used: Head

    Destroys one wraith. Deals ranged INT-based fire damage to all enemies.

    Has no speed modifier and a base accuracy of 150% at all levels.
